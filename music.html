About Sierra

Sierra Carrère is a multifaceted artist, performer, and creator based in Maui. With a voice that captivates and a presence that inspires, she brings passion and authenticity to everything she creates.

Her music blends soulful melodies with heartfelt lyrics, exploring themes of self-love, growth, and connection. Whether on stage or in the studio, Sierra's artistry reflects her journey of embracing life fully.
